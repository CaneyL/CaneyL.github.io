The Story of the Lotus-Eater

About
& Mission

At Lōtos, we immerse ourselves in the art of scent, drawing inspiration from the timeless allure of the Lotus-eaters from antiquity, and the unhurried elegance of 19th-century Seine riverbanks. Rooted in Edmonton, Alberta, we are creators of olfactory landscapes meticulously crafted with premium natural ingredients. We reject conformity and embrace the revolutionary spirit of indulgent idealism, inviting you to live free from the pressures of modern society.

Our Lotus-eater is a visionary artist and romantic soul. Surrounded by the fragrant blooms that inspire Lōtos’ scents, they leisurely craft letters to a distant lover while painting tales of love and leisure on canvas.

Our Values

We create fragrances that are organic, vegan and made from the highest quality, sustainably sourced, natural ingredients. Working with the rhythms of the seasons, we blend, barrel-age and bottle all of our scents.
